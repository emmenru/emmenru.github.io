EMMA FRID



ABOUT

Hi, I am Emma, a researcher and engineer specialized in music technology and accessibility. I use sound and data to create engaging and accessible user experiences. I have a PhD in Sound and Music Computing from KTH Royal Institute of Technology and bring over 10 years of experience creating interactive audio technologies across research, education, and artistic applications. I have previously worked at IRCAM and Adobe; taught courses in audio programming, multimodal interface design and statistics; and consulted for companies looking to enhance user experience through research and data-driven sound design. To get in touch, please feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn!
